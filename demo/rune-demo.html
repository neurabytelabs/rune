Architecture

The 8 Layers of RUNE

L0 · SYSTEM CORE

Role & Persona

Defines who the AI is for this task — expertise level, behavioral rules, and foundational identity constraints.

L1 · CONTEXT IDENTITY

Domain & Audience

Anchors the AI in the right knowledge domain and specifies exactly who the output is for.

L2 · INTENT SCOPE

Task Definition

Crystallizes the precise goal, expected deliverables, and output format — eliminating ambiguity.

L3 · GOVERNANCE

Constraints & Ethics

Sets hard and soft limits — what to avoid, what's out of scope, and ethical boundaries to respect.

L4 · COGNITIVE ENGINE

Thinking Strategy

Instructs the AI how to reason — chain-of-thought, tree-of-thought, step-by-step decomposition.

L5 · CAPABILITIES

Tools & Resources

Declares what tools, data sources, and external capabilities are available for the task.

L6 · QUALITY ASSURANCE

Validation Criteria

Defines the success criteria — how the AI should self-validate before producing the final output.

L7 · OUTPUT META

Format & Style

Controls the final form — length, language, reading level, structure, and presentation format.
